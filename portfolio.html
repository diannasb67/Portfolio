Fam.ily

Our vision was to create a social media application for family members with user authentication and three layer approval process for family members wanting to to join a family tree. Our aim was to create a safe and secure platform for family members to chat with each other and to create and view events using a shared calendar. The application also allows members to share family contact information via virtual address book and share wish list between family members for gift ideas for birthdays and celebrations.

  • User can create or join a family tree.
  • Chat with other family members in the chat room.
  • Users can look up their family members contact information and wish list if they want to send a gift or birthday card.
  • Users can create, view and edit events such as birthdays, anniversaries, weddings, etc.
